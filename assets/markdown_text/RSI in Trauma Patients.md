# RSI in Trauma Patients
### Stable Patient
Fentanyl 3micrograms/kg
Ketamine 2mg/kg
Rocuronium 1mg/kg

### Unstable Patient
Fentanyl 1micrograms/kg
Ketamine 1mg/kg
Rocuronium 1mg/kg

Note: At anesthetists discretion suxamethonium may be used. Suxamethonium dose 1-2mg/kg IV/ IO (4mg/kg IM)
